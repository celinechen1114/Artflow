Help
Drawing Tool:

Click on the canvas and drag your cursor to draw.

Eraser Tool:

Click on the canvas and drag your cursor to erase.

Black & White Filter:

Applies a black and white filter to the entire canvas.

Sepia Filter:

Applies a sepia filter to the entire canvas.

Tritanopia Filter:

Applies a Tritanopia filter to the entire canvas, simulating how a color blind person would see the drawing.
